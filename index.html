Medicine, with help from technology

This is a simple website about the uses of technology in the medicinal field. Throughout all of time, medicine has evolved along the side of technology and as technology advances, so will medicine. This website is meant to show you just how much technology is in the medicinal field.

Ultrasound machines use sound waves to produce images in your body.

An artificial heart is a pump placed in the chest to replace damaged heart valves.

PET scanners show biochemical fuction in the organs and tissues.

X-ray machines let people see your bones by using waves of electromagnetic radiation.

Patient moniters are used to let your doctors and nurses see your heart rate, body temperature, and blood pressure.

Electrocardiograms are used to measure the electroactivity

Anesthesia machines deliver oxygen and remove carbon dioxide

Sphygmomanometers are used to measure blood pressure.
